Announcement

Announce it like you mean it.

Today, I’m excited to announce the launch of Like Me, a true competitor to Facebook. For too long, social media has been cluttered by user interaction. Comments create noise. Messages create expectations. Friends create complexity. Opinions distract from what really matters. Ads. At Like Me, we believe user interaction is disruptive to the advertising experience. Frankly, it is disrespectful to the ads. So we built a platform around a simpler vision: no posting, no commenting, no chatting, no communities, no messy self-expression. Just ads, and the purest remaining form of human agency: liking ads. On Like Me, users can finally express themselves thoroughly and authentically by engaging directly with sponsored content. Not through conversation, but through alignment. Not through dialogue, but through preference signaling. Not through connection, but through tasteful appreciation of premium messaging. This is not just the future of social media. It is the purification of it. We are removing the social from social media so the media can finally breathe. More soon. #startup #socialmedia #advertising #product #innovation #april1
